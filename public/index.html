The Quest For Wonders: The Hidden Stone Beneath the Sea is a magical underwater adventure that invites young readers into a world of mystery, courage, and imagination.

When Mia and Jake discover a mysterious map, they are drawn into an unforgettable journey beneath the ocean's surface. Surrounded by colorful sea life and hidden pathways, they uncover secrets that have been waiting for generations. But as they search for the hidden stone, they begin to realize that the greatest discoveries come from bravery, curiosity, and believing in themselves.

This beautifully illustrated children's book brings the ocean to life with vivid imagery and engaging storytelling, making it perfect for young readers who love adventure, exploration, and magical discoveries.

📝 Curriculum Alignment: Meeting Your Educational Goals

Common Core State Standards (CCSS) — ELA & Literacy

The Quest for Wonders develops critical literacy skills through evidence-based reading, comprehension strategies, and rich academic vocabulary embedded in meaningful narrative contexts.

The story seamlessly introduces Tier 2 and Tier 3 academic vocabulary covering marine biology and global geography, supporting vocabulary acquisition aligned with reading informational text standards.

Through structured comprehension activities, students practise asking and answering questions, identifying main idea, and citing textual evidence — foundational skills across all grade bands.

Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS) — Earth & Life Sciences

The Hidden Stone quest fosters scientific curiosity through hands-on exploration of earth and life sciences. Students investigate real-world phenomena encountered by Mia and Jake, applying the three dimensions of NGSS: Science & Engineering Practices, Disciplinary Core Ideas, and Crosscutting Concepts.

Core concepts include ocean ecosystems and biodiversity, light filtration through water, underwater pressure, food webs, and adaptation of marine organisms — each anchored to specific performance expectations below.
